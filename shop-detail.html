Skincare involves a series of practices that support skin integrity, enhance its appearance, and relieve skin conditions. These practices range from simple hygiene, such as washing the face, to the use of various products like moisturizers, serums, and sunscreens.

A basic skincare routine typically involves the following steps:
1. Cleansing: Removes dirt, oil, and impurities from the skin. It's essential to use a gentle cleanser that matches your skin type.
2. Exfoliating: Removes dead skin cells and helps to keep the pores clear. Exfoliation should be done 1-2 times a week to avoid irritation.
3. Toning: Balances the skin's pH and prepares it for the next steps. Toners can also help to tighten pores and remove any leftover impurities.
4. Moisturizing: Keeps the skin hydrated and locks in moisture. Moisturizers are crucial for all skin types, even oily skin.
